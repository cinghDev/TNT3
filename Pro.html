Powerful Features

Multi-Format Support

Play M3U, M3U8, Xtream Codes, Stalker Portal, and direct stream URLs. Support for 300,000+ channels per playlist.

Advanced Video Player

Custom aspect ratios, playback speed control, subtitle support, audio track selection, and gesture controls.

DRM & Security

Full support for Widevine and ClearKey DRM. Encrypted playlist backup and restore with .rubyp format.

EPG Support
