Multi-Format Support

M3U, M3U8, Xtream Codes, Stalker Portal, and direct URLs. Handle playlists with 300,000+ channels effortlessly.

Advanced Player

Custom aspect ratios, playback speed, subtitle support, audio tracks, and intuitive gesture controls.

DRM & Security

Full Widevine and ClearKey DRM support. Encrypted playlist backup with secure .rubyp format.
